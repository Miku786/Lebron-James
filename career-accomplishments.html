Career and Accomplishments

LeBron entered the NBA in 2003 as the first overall pick by the Cleveland Cavaliers. Over his career, he has won four NBA championships, earned four MVP awards, and been selected to numerous All-Star teams. He has played for the Cavaliers, Miami Heat, and Los Angeles Lakers, constantly redefining the role of a modern basketball player.
